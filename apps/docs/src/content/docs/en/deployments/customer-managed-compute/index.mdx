title: Customer managed compute
description: Runners, custom regions, and compute you operate in your infrastructure.
---

**Customer managed compute** means you attach and operate runner machines in **custom regions** so sandboxes run on infrastructure you control. Shared and dedicated Daytona regions use Daytona-managed runners; custom regions use your runners and your operational practices.

Read the full guide at **[Customer managed compute](/docs/en/runners)** (runners and custom regions; same page as before, linked here for navigation under **Deployments**).

:::caution
Custom runners are experimental and may change. To request access, contact [support@daytona.io](mailto:support@daytona.io).
:::
