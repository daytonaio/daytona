---
title: Platform Management
description: Account-level administration for organizations, access, usage limits, and billing in Daytona.
---

Platform management covers how you administer a Daytona account at the organization level: who can authenticate, how work is grouped, what resources you may consume, and how usage is billed. Most tasks are available in the [Daytona Dashboard ↗](https://app.daytona.io/dashboard/) and through the [Daytona API](/docs/en/tools/api/) where endpoints exist for the same concepts.

- **[API keys](/docs/en/api-keys)**
- **[Organizations](/docs/en/organizations)**
- **[Limits](/docs/en/limits)**
- **[Billing](/docs/en/billing)**

Together, these areas define how your team authenticates, which organization context applies to each request, what you can run before hitting limits, and how that usage maps to spend.
