Use this section to administer your Daytona organization: credentials, membership, usage limits, and billing. The guides below live at the same URLs as before; they are grouped here under **Platform management** in the sidebar.

## Account management

### [API Keys](/docs/en/api-keys)

Create and manage API keys for your organization, including scopes and rotation. Keys authenticate requests to the [Daytona API](/docs/en/tools/api) and are used by the [SDKs](/docs/en/getting-started#sdks) and [CLI](/docs/en/getting-started#cli).

### [Organizations](/docs/en/organizations)

Create and manage [organizations](/docs/en/organizations), including members and resources attached to each organization.

### [Limits](/docs/en/limits)

Review limits and tiers assigned to your organization, including usage caps relevant to sandboxes and API access.

### [Billing](/docs/en/billing)

Manage billing for your organization, including plans and payment-related settings.
