Daytona supports observability through **OpenTelemetry**: traces, logs, and metrics from sandboxes, optional organization-level metrics export, and SDK-side tracing when you enable the experimental `otelEnabled` flag.

The full guide lives at **[OpenTelemetry collection](/docs/en/experimental/otel-collection)** (same URL as before; it is grouped here under **Manage** for navigation).

:::caution
OpenTelemetry collection is experimental and may change. To request access, contact [support@daytona.io](mailto:support@daytona.io).
:::
